Larisa Nekrasova

«If you want to learn more and grow professionally and personally — become the facilitator; if you want to make others grow and develop — become a passionate facilitator!»

Larisa has been involved in Learning, Development and Education since 2001. She worked as an Associate Professor in Herzen State Pedagogical University of Russia in St. Petersburg, and she currently works as a certified facilitator in Learning and Development Sector in the Middle East, Europe and Asia.

Larisa is a certified facilitator and provides trainings that cover topics like: Effective Communication Skills, Behavior (Psychometric) Assessment, Presentation Skills, Motivation Skills, Situational Leadership, Effective Customer Relationships, Working Efficiently in Teams, Fundamentals of Finance, Project Management. She is also capable of identifying the training and development needs of departments or organizations then designing training solutions that serve their needs.

Larisa holds a Ph.D. in Philology; has more than 15 publications as well as the published monograph «The Stylistic Functions of Historic Words»; participated in numerous international research conferences and symposiums; took part in the multi-regional project «Contemporary Challenges in U.S. Higher Education» sponsored by the State Department of the U.S.A.; has an extensive background in teaching practical and theoretical aspects of the English language to different age groups. Larisa played an important role in the course curriculum redesign within the Bologna educational reform in Russia.Larisa is very passionate about training and she considers it a great opportunity to connect and share knowledge and expertise with people. She targets on creating dynamic, eye opening, highly interactive, and fun educational experiences for diverse groups to constantly exceed their highest expectations.

Larisa has traveled and facilitated trainings in more than 10 countries within Europe, Middle East, Asia, Africa and Australia. She is a highly motivated Training Facilitator capable of delivering sessions in English and Russian.
